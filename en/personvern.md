Privacy Policy for VetKort

Last updated: 19.07.2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VetKort processes your and your pet’s personal data when you use our app.

2. What Information Do We Collect?

We only collect information that is necessary for the app to function as intended:

User information: Email address when registering an account.
Pet information: Name, breed, microchip number, and images you choose to upload.
Health data: Veterinary visits, vaccination status, medications, and medical notes.
Documents and images: Images of vaccination records, veterinary invoices, insurance documents, and text information extracted from these.
Technical data: Information related to subscription status and app usage in order to provide the service.
3. How Do We Use the Information?

The information is used exclusively to provide you with an overview of your pet’s health, generate vaccination certificates, and send notifications about important events. We do not use your data for marketing purposes directed at third parties.

AI services: For automated data entry, we use reputable AI partners for text recognition (OCR). Documents are sent in encrypted form, and our partners are contractually obligated not to use your documents or data to train their machine learning models.
4. Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely. We use Firebase (Google) as a platform for authentication, database services, and storage.

Security: Access to your data is limited to you as the user. We do not have access to your specific health data beyond what is strictly necessary for system operations or support requested by you.
Encryption: Uploaded image files and documents are stored encrypted. Access to these files is restricted to your unique user identifier.
5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. At any time, you can:

View all information we have stored about you and your pets.
Edit or update your information.
Delete data and account: You can delete individual images or documents at any time within the app. When you delete a document, or choose to permanently delete your entire account through the app settings, all associated image files and data are permanently and immediately removed from our cloud servers.
6. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding privacy or your data, please contact us through the app or send an email to kjetil.teigen92@gmail.com.
